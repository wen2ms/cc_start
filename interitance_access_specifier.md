### Key Differences:

| **Access Specifier** | **Accessible Inside Class** | **Accessible in Derived Classes** | **Accessible from Outside Class** |
|----------------------|-----------------------------|------------------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| **Public**            | Yes                         | Yes                                | Yes                               |
| **Protected**         | Yes                         | Yes                                | No                                |
| **Private**           | Yes                         | No                                 | No                                |
